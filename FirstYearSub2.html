  • Major Selection

    • When it comes to major selection, the most important tip is to discover your inner passsion for what you really love and want to pursue. You may have heard some people talking about the "jobs that achieve the fastest growth in the recent five years" or "jobs with highest average salary" on many reviews or websites, but what you really need to do is to ignore those information and remember what YOU like and would love to study.
